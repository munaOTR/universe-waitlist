Common Questions

When will UniVerse ICOS launch?
We're currently in active development. Waitlist members will be the first to receive access. Join the waitlist to stay updated on our launch timeline.
Which universities will be on the platform first?
We're starting with select federal universities including UNILAG, UI, FUTA, and ABU. More schools will be onboarded progressively.
How do I report a scam or fraudulent listing?
Send us a WhatsApp message immediately with full details. Mark the subject as [URGENT - FRAUD REPORT] if emailing. We act on reports within 24 hours.
Can my university partner with UniVerse ICOS?
Yes! We actively welcome university partnerships. Send us a message using the "University Onboarding" category above and our team will respond within 48 hours.
