SECTION 01

Introduction

🌐
Welcome to UniVerse ICOS — the Integrated Campus Operating System. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our student users while providing a secure environment for academic and commercial transactions.

This Privacy Policy explains how UniVerse ICOS Limited ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data across all 7 Pillars of our platform — including the Study Hub, Errand Page, Lodge Finder, Skill Place, Market Place, and Premium Dashboard.

By accessing or using the UniVerse platform, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully and reach out to our Compliance Officer with any questions.

SECTION 02

Information We Collect

To deliver our mission as Nigeria's first Integrated Campus Operating System — a National Asset — we collect the following categories of data to power a secure, personalized experience:

  • 🪪
    Identity Data
    Your name, institutional email address, and student ID number — used for verification on university dashboards and to confirm your status as an enrolled student.
  • 💳
    Transaction Data
    Details of errands posted or completed, skills hired via Skill Place, and marketplace purchases — used to facilitate our secure escrow and dispute resolution system.
  • 📚
    Academic Data
    Your course selections — such as GST 111, STA 111, and MTH 101 — to personalize your Study Hub experience with relevant past questions and AI-powered preparation tools.
  • 📍
    Location Data
    Campus-specific location data to help you discover nearby verified lodges, connect with local errand runners, and access campus-native vendors on the marketplace.
SECTION 03

How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to power the UniVerse ecosystem. Your data is never used for purposes outside of improving your campus experience:

  • ✅
    Verification & Trust
    Ensuring that hostel listings, service providers, and errand runners are legitimate, enrolled students — protecting you from scams and bad actors on campus.
  • ⚡
    Platform Performance
    Optimizing the app experience for entry-level hardware, including 4GB RAM devices commonly used by Nigerian students, ensuring fast load times and a smooth interface.
  • 🛡️
    Regional Security
    Applying regional logic to restrict or permit specific banking and transaction functions in compliance with Nigerian financial regulations and university-specific policies.
SECTION 04

Data Sharing & Security

The security of your data is foundational to our mission. We apply industry-leading infrastructure and strict no-sale policies to keep your information safe.

🚫
No Third-Party Sales We do not sell your personal data to advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties. Ever.
🗄️
Supabase Infrastructure All data is stored on Supabase with high-level encryption and row-level security (RLS) protocols.
💰
Escrow Payment Safety Financial data is handled via secure processors — Paystack or Flutterwave — and is never stored on our servers directly.
🔐
End-to-End Encryption Sensitive identity and transaction data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 standards.
SECTION 05

Your Rights

As a member of the UniVerse Orbit, you hold full ownership of your data. We respect and uphold the following rights at all times:

  • ✦ Access & Update: You may view and update your profile information at any time through your account settings.
  • ✦ Right to Deletion: You may request the full deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting our Compliance Officer.
  • ✦ Opt-Out of Marketing: You may opt out of non-essential marketing communications from our Growth Director at any time via your notification preferences.
  • ✦ Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • ✦ Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.
