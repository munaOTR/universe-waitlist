The 7 Pillars & User Conduct

All users agree to use the following platform modules in a professional, honest, and lawful manner. Violations are subject to immediate suspension or permanent account termination.

📦
The Errand Page
Users must fulfill errand tasks exactly as described and agreed upon. Failure to deliver without valid cause, or misappropriating funds held in escrow, may result in permanent suspension and legal referral.
📚
The Study Hub
All academic materials accessed through the Study Hub — including past questions, AI-generated content, and peer notes — are for personal use only. Redistribution for commercial gain is strictly prohibited.
💡
Skill Place
Service listings (e.g., barbing, writing, design, coding) must be honest, accurate, and free of fraudulent claims. Misrepresentation of skills or qualifications is grounds for immediate removal.

Vendors must list only items they legitimately own or have authority to sell. Counterfeit goods, prohibited items, or listings designed to defraud buyers will result in permanent vendor ban.

🌐
The Orbit & Premium Dashboard
Users must not attempt to exploit, reverse-engineer, or circumvent the dashboard's access controls. Unauthorized access to another student's portal data is a criminal offence under Nigerian cybercrime law.
SECTION 04

The Secure Escrow System

To ensure the trillion-naira potential of the Nigerian student economy is protected, UniVerse operates a secure, multi-stage payment protocol for all transactions on the Errand Page, Skill Place, and Marketplace.

1
Payment Holding
Funds for errands or skill services are held securely by UniVerse ICOS and are not released to the service provider until the task is confirmed complete by both parties.
2
Dual Confirmation
Both the requester and the service provider must confirm completion before funds are disbursed. In cases of one-sided confirmation, a 24-hour review window is triggered automatically.
3
Dispute Resolution
The Operations & Logistics Manager serves as the final arbiter in any unresolved transaction disputes. Their decision, informed by platform evidence (chat logs, delivery confirmation), is binding on all parties.
4
Regional Restrictions
Access to certain financial features may be restricted based on regional security logic and Nigerian banking regulations, in order to protect the integrity of the transaction network.
⚠️
Fraud Warning: Any attempt to manipulate the escrow system, collude to file false disputes, or extract funds through deceptive means will result in permanent account termination, full fund recovery, and potential criminal reporting to the EFCC and Nigerian Police Force Cybercrime Unit.
